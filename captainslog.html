Captain's Log Entry #1

August 13, 2021 12:50 PM

     Today is my first day as an upperclassman. Freshman year was hell, Sophomore year sucked, I hope Junior year can be a lot better. I don't know what will be in store but my classes aren't that bad so far. I've got a composition class followed up by a drawing class at the start of the day, so not too shabby. I've got some opps in my culinary class but it's nothing I can't handle. I got to do weights in PE, hopefully I get more chances to lift, but based on what the gym teacher told me, it's probably gonna be a sporty hell.

     I've got B lunch and most of my friends are at C lunch. I sent an email to the scheduling lady and hopefully we can get that fixed. I'm in Algebra II right now, pretty run of the mill. After this I go to choir, then world history, and then I'll be on my way back home. In reality my schedule isn't bad, I just wasn't ready for summer to end so abruptly. I'm gonna work on music when I get home unless I get too tired and end up taking a nap. It's Friday, so I can do whatever I want over the weekend.

     Maybe getting back in the school groove isn't that bad. I just want to have enough time for music and possibly a job, and really I don't wanna suffer like I did last year. I've got high hopes for this year, and big plans for this page, I'll keep y'all updated whenever I can. What sucks is every time I close my school Chromebook that I'm writing this on, it erases whatever I've written. This is take three.


Captain’s Log Entry #2

August 16, 2021 10:29


    I finally got smart and put this on a Google Doc, I’ll just implement it into the webpage later. Today is my second day as an upperclassman, to be honest it isn’t that bad. I finished up my composition assignment, drew a name sticker for art, and got my locker for gym class. Apparently we have to bring our own clothes, which I wasn’t aware of. I guess the school is too lazy to fork us over gym shorts and shirts like they did since the dawn of time. 

    I go to study hall in five minutes, so I’ll probably have more time to write some more junk here. I’m sort of tired but I know I can’t go to sleep until later or I’ll mess up my sleep schedule. I was up late last night working on The Yung Ulcer EP. It’s sounding good, good enough that another month of continuous work will make it adequate for the September 11 release date. My biggest concern now is surviving and adapting my sleep schedule to fit school. I want to work out a lot, and make good music. It’s at the point that as soon as I finish TYUE I’ll be starting on my next project. Not much more work to do, just mixing and mastering and all that. 

    I’ll get to work on producing music videos soon. I’d like one to come out on release day and then the rest will come out thoroughly. I’m not sure which songs will get the most views, and it’ll partially depend on what bullshit I do. The thing is, I really didn’t do anything special for the KING OF THE HILL or I KEEP MY HANDS SANITIZED videos, it’s just a rap video. For the Ulcer EP stuff I’m gonna be a complete dick out in public. I’ll trespass on property and find more animal carasses. I know I wanna wear an 80’s punk outfit for the first track, my freshman year getup for track two, and I’m gonna make a scripted film sort of experience to go along with the storytelling of track three. 

    Cohesively as a project, Ulcer EP is great. It’s a mixtape of sorts with songs old and new. One of the tracks was written back in 2019 while I was drunk on a pontoon boat. There’s no cohesive story that spans the whole EP but a few songs tell stories and share ideas. There’s lots of cussing and random garbage that’ll never get played on the radio. And that’s okay! This is the project I made for people to look back on when I’m releasing grander, more extravagant stuff. It’s a starting point but in a way it’s also the end of an era. I didn’t put a lot of thought into many lyrics and you’ll be able to tell the difference between my efforts when the next project drops.

    The last ‘real’ project I put out was Evergreen, a colorful beat tape of sorts. It’s a juxtaposition of Yung Ulcer music. The beats were psychedelic and nostalgic, the instruments played gentle melodies. I’d love to rap or sing on these types of instrumentals and that’s my plan for the next project. September comes, Ulcer EP drops with some funny music videos, then I’m in real shit mode. In either November or December, you can expect to hear Dirty Blonde. I’ll sing on the project, and it’s not very silly as much as it is mindful. It’s a testament to my thoughts and experiences that have made me into the person I am today. The first track is a piano ballad about nostalgia for virtual landscapes, places that have grown familiar and never change. It’s hipster indie junk, you’ll love it. I gotta go to lunch soon. I wonder if I can implement all this crap into the site before the bell rings.


Captain’s Log Entry #3

August 17, 2021

    I woke up at 5:30, tired as hell. I took a hot shower to try and get some energy. I got dressed, brushed my teeth, and made some eggs. Part of the whites fell out of the pan and landed on the burner and it kinda smelled bad. I would’ve cleaned it up but I had to eat and be out the door, I put on my black forces and got on the bus. The bus ride was dissociative but also relaxing, I was listening to the new Mild High Club song and it put on a good mix of psychedelic lofi sounding shit afterwards. Listening to completely new music is way better than my Spotify liked songs. In the first hour we watched a bit of Terry Tate: Office Linebacker. It’s super funny. I had to write a prompt about if he worked at my school and I did a decent job.

    In the second hour, I mostly just doodled and waited for the slow ass writers to pass down the vocabulary sheet. After that I went to PE, changed, stretched, and lifted weights. I was so exhausted afterwards, so I headed back to my locker for my water bottle. I accidentally went to my study hall class instead of culinary, but I rushed back and made something up about issues with my locker. We took a pretest, now I’m hungry and ready to go to lunch in a bit. Chances are I’ll spend the next period implementing these excerpts into the diary.

    I was up late last night, and it was both worth it and not. I made a pretty fantastic beat that I’m gonna try and rap over when I get home. I sampled an old country song and gave it trap drums. It’ll be a masterpiece. I only got around five hours of sleep, the night before it was three. I’ll be off to bed early tonight for sure, man I hate being tired. The highlight of my day has been my composition teacher showing us Terry Tate.
