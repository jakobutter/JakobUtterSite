The Tall Evergreen Tree

By Jakob Utter


His name tells how it was with him.

He was a great big evergreen tree

Swaying back and forth in the wind

With strong branches reaching out

In all different directions.

Whether the sun scorched down

Or hid away in a blizzard,

He stood sturdy in the ground

And continued to grow.

He grew extraordinarily tall

Taller than all the other trees in the forest

Watching some crash down and others sprout up.

Sometimes he would feel heavy

And branches would fall

As he was unsure of which way to grow.

Still the evergreen tree stood tall

With its roots burrowing deep into the earth

And became a lot stronger with time.
