AVOIDANCE




 

I wake up

take my stance

boot up some old game


spend all day

doing something gray

that won't push me to fame


I want to cry

and pass this shame

but there's no one to blame


I've tried so much

to make a change

but I still feel the same


I'm not quite sure just what I want

I want to ease the pain

thought a sober mind would do me good

but now my thoughts are tame

I'm from a line of wasted minds

I wish to break the chain

but I'm not strong enough for loss

and just too weak for gain


I used to love creating songs

but lately not too much

all the things that made me me

have fallen out of touch

I could overthink so much

and contemplate on why

but I don't see the point in things

sometimes I want to die


Is that a normal thought to have

for someone still so young?

I fear that I might meet an end

though life has just begun

I know that I've never fit in

I do what works for me

for years I made a grave mistake

inhaling THC

I'm off it now, it's for the best

set in a sober mind

looking back and all I find

Is lots of wasted time


Often scared I've lost the spark

So then what would I do?

sometimes my mind will tell me things

I'm just scared that they're true


I know that soon I will leave home

the fear has just set in

I set the bar, it's way too high

and my chances are thin

I'm not sure where I could start

I'm scared of reaching ends

I've never met someone like me

through many years of friends


I know if my attempts succeed

that it was for the best

often feeling tired

I could put myself to rest

and I don't have enough willpower

to get on the right track

I'm terrified that I'll cave in

and life will fade to black
